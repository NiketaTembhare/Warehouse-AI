SLOTTING POLICY - WAREHOUSE STORAGE GUIDELINES

1. High velocity SKUs (Class A) must always be stored in Zone A near packing station.
2. Medium velocity SKUs (Class B) are stored in Zone B.
3. Low velocity SKUs (Class C) are stored in Zone C furthest from packing.
4. Fragile items must be stored on lower shelves below 1.5 meters height.
5. Heavy items above 15kg must be stored at ground level only.
6. No re-slotting is permitted without manager digital approval in the system.
7. All bin moves must be logged in the slotting history with reason and timestamp.
8. Re-slotting should be scheduled during Night shift to avoid disruption.
9. Cold storage items must never be moved to ambient temperature zones.
