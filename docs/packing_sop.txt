PACKING SOP - WAREHOUSE STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE

1. Verify pick list against physical items before packing begins.
2. Fragile items must be wrapped in bubble wrap before placing in box.
3. Heavy items (above 10kg) must always be packed at the bottom of the box.
4. Maximum box weight limit is 25kg. Split into two boxes if exceeded.
5. Include packing slip inside every box before sealing.
6. Apply shipping label clearly on the largest flat surface of the box.
7. Priority orders must be packed and dispatched within 1 hour of picking.
8. All packed orders must be scanned as Packed in WMS before moving to dispatch.
