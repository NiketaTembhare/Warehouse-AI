RECEIVING SOP - WAREHOUSE STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE

1. All incoming shipments must be inspected within 2 hours of arrival.
2. Verify item count against purchase order before unloading.
3. Damaged items during receiving must be photographed and tagged immediately.
4. Damaged goods must be moved to Zone D (damaged holding area) within 2 hours.
5. Create a damage report with supplier name, item details, and damage description.
6. Notify the warehouse manager immediately for damaged shipments above 10 units.
7. All received items must be scanned into the WMS before leaving the receiving dock.
8. Temperature-sensitive items must be moved to cold storage within 30 minutes.
