WAREHOUSE SAFETY GUIDELINES

1. All workers must wear safety shoes and high-visibility vest at all times.
2. Maximum forklift speed inside warehouse is 10 km/h.
3. Pedestrian zones are marked in yellow - forklifts must stop and yield.
4. Chemical products must never be stored near food or pharmaceutical items.
5. Fire exits must remain clear at all times - no inventory blocking exits.
6. Workers must not operate forklifts without a valid forklift certification.
7. Maximum shelf height for manual picking is 2.5 meters without equipment.
8. Report any unsafe condition immediately to the shift supervisor.
9. Spills must be cleaned up immediately with appropriate materials.
10. First aid kit is located at the entrance of each zone.
